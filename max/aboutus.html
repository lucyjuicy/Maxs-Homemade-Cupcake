About Us

-- Lucy and Eva

Four years ago, I was attracted by the TV series -- 2 Broke Girls. The girls in the show are so funny and optimistic. But what attracted me more is the cupcake in the show. As I soon met my business partner, who also loved cupcakes so much, that we decided to start our business.

What makes us unique -- we make and sell the cupcakes appeared in 2 Broke Girls. We did a lot of investigation on the raw materials and the skills on baking. And then we started our own bakery. We selected the raw material carefully. Different from other bakeries, we can offer nearly 100% similar cupcakes to the people who also like the show, or the cupcakes, or both of them.

Our main distribution channel is selling online. As a customer place an order online, we will deliver the cupcakes to the order address. This kind of selling will decrease a lot of capital on the decorating the bakery and on employee hiring. Hence, instead of have more subbranches, we plan to put more fund on advertisement to get larger popularity.

We are also looking for business partners or investment, And the partners will share the profits every year. If you have the same interests and passions, please feel free to contact us.
